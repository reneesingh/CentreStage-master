Recording artist and band leader

Rob James is one of Western Canada's premier solo recording artists. He has extensive experience in recording and live performances of both original and cover tunes. His styles range from rock and country to love ballads. His CD's are all self-produced. He composes and plays all instruments on his CDs himself. Even the cover art is his design.

Rob sets time aside to work on soundtracks and film scores. He can even be commissioned to write songs on any subject for anyone, no matter what the occasion is. With his state-of-the-art recording studio (128-Track Digital) and access to any sound, the possibilities are endless! So if you need a rocking performer or a special song for your loved one, look no further! Rob can meet all your needs!!

James was born in Vancouver, and raised in North Vancouver, B.C. He began his musical career by listening to the radio and picking up his favorite songs. He then went on to sing for some local rock bands in Vancouver and gained valuable live performance experience. Playing in local downtown Vancouver clubs he learned to love connecting with the audience! And, to this very day is still bitten by the performing bug! Rob James is also available for audio consulting, instrument lessons and audio engineering courses, one-on-one or group sessions!
